#### Element Type: Altered

#### Altered Resonance
Increase ATK by 20% and altered resistance by 30%. Activate by equipping 2 or more altered weapons. This set effect works in the off-hand slot. Cannot stack with other Altered Resonance or Altered Reaction effects, with Altered Resonance taking precedence.


#### Advancements

1★ Using any weapon or discharge skill grants 1 elemental crystal, up to 5 crystals. Elemental crystals are lost at a rate of 1 crystal every 6 seconds.
Elemental crystals provide a final damage boost equal to 4% of ((crystal count plus 1)/2) and an increased healing effect equal to 6% of ((crystal count plus 1)/2).

3★ While Benediction Resonance is not active, when the target's HP is below (crystal count plus 2)%, using Moonstar Bracelet can instantly execute the target (doesn't work against Wanderers).
While Benediction Resonance is active, when the target's HP is below (crystal count times 2 plus 2)%, using Moonstar Bracelet can instantly execute the target (doesn't work against Wanderers).

5★ Obtain 1 elemental crystal every time Scatter Technique is used. Elemental crystals are lost at a rate of 1 crystal every 20 seconds.
When receiving fatal damage, consume 3 elemental crystals to prevent death 1 time and gain damage immunity for a short time. Can be activated 1 time every 25 seconds.

6★ Increase final damage by 7% of ((crystal count plus 3)/2).
